BECHT - Precision Design for a Specialized Market

This project focused on creating two roll-up banners for an exclusive distributor store in Algeria representing Alfred Becht GmbH, a German brand known for high-quality cleaning and hygiene solutions, particularly in dental and professional environments.

Visit the company's website

1. The Objective - Communicate Trust and Clarity

The goal was to design banners that clearly communicate product reliability, hygiene standards, and professional quality. Given the context, medical and everyday cleaning, the visuals needed to feel clean, structured, and trustworthy.

2. Design Approach - Minimal, Functional, Effective

I focused on a clear visual hierarchy so key information is instantly readable in a physical space. The design balanced clinical cleanliness through whites and spacing, brand alignment with Becht's identity, and visual impact for in-store visibility.

3. Working Within Constraints

The challenge was to design within strict brand guidelines and limited space while still creating something visually engaging. Every element, from typography to spacing, was carefully considered to maximize clarity and professionalism.

4. Final Output - Physical Presence

The final result consisted of two roll-up banners designed for real-world use, enhancing the store's presence and reinforcing the brand's credibility at the point of sale.

Closing

This project highlights the importance of precision and restraint, where strong design is not about complexity, but about clarity, trust, and purpose.
